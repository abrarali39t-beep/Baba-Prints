Our Story

Crafting Brands That Endure

Welcome to Baba Advertisers Srinagar — a full-service advertising and branding studio powered by Baba Prints, founded by Mohammad Yamin Baba with a singular vision: to bring world-class creativity and print quality to the heart of Kashmir.

Over more than a decade, we have grown from a passionate one-man operation into a comprehensive branding powerhouse trusted by hundreds of businesses across Jammu & Kashmir. Our journey is defined by an unyielding commitment to quality, creativity, and client satisfaction.

Whether it's a startup's first business card or a corporation's massive event branding campaign, we approach every project with the same enthusiasm and dedication that has become our trademark.
